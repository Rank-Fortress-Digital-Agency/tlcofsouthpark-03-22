Welcome back!

This week, our toddlers are working on a garden project. They will discover what different vegetables, fruit and flowers grow in a garden, and what does a gardener do to take care of a garden. They will also find out what animals and insects live and eat in a garden. Then they will plant their own flowers and paint different pictures of what their garden should look like.

All of our children are still practicing very hard for a very special song Fest for their families. Upcoming soon!
